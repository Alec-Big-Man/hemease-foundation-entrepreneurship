About Us

Our Story

HemEase started with a simple problem faced by six college friends: the struggle of finding a convenient tailoring shop. Tailoring clothes cost too much money over time, and also can be a massive waste of time if you have to go out of your way to find a shop. That's why we started HemEase, to make sure all our tailor and dry cleaning needs can be done at home.
